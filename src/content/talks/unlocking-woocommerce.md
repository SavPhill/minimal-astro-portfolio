## Efficiency is the only way to scale.

At our May 2025 meetup, we did something different. Instead of a lecture, we ran a live "Help-Desk." The room was full of shop owners struggling with the same thing: manual tasks.

Whether you're running a small boutique or a high-volume resort store, if you are manually entering data or chasing payments, you aren't a business owner—you’re a clerk.

### Key Takeaways:
* **Hidden Gems:** We explored lesser-known plugins that handle automated tax reconciliation and inventory syncing.
* **Direct Booking Logic:** We discussed how to bypass the "OTA tax" by optimizing the native WooCommerce checkout for speed.
* **The AI Layer:** Even back then, we were looking at how AI agents could handle basic customer service queries directly within the shop environment.

Scale doesn't come from working harder; it comes from untangling the tech so the machine does the work for you.
