## Stop paying the "Builder Tax."

In April 2025, I led a hands-on session at WordPress Chiang Mai focused on **Full Site Editing (FSE)**. The room was divided: half were die-hard Elementor fans, the other half were tired of slow loading speeds and constant plugin updates.

My argument was simple: If you want **AI Visibility**, you need speed. AI crawlers don't want to dig through layers of heavy shortcodes; they want clean, semantic HTML.

### Why FSE is the "Architect's" Choice:
* **Zero Bloat:** It’s native to WordPress. No extra CSS libraries slowing down your Largest Contentful Paint (LCP).
* **Intuitive Logic:** Designing with blocks is closer to how modern web architecture actually works.
* **Security:** Fewer third-party builders means a smaller attack surface for the vulnerabilities I frequently document in my [Security Research](/security).

We built a professional landing page from scratch in under an hour. No custom code, just smart use of the native block system. 

**The takeaway:** High performance isn't about what you add; it's about what you remove.
