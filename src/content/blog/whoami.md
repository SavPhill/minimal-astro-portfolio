## Hi, I'm Phill.

After years of navigating the intersection of business and technology, I decided it was time to move my thoughts from private notebooks to a public garden.

I’m a **Digital Transformation Consultant**. In plain English, that means I help organizations stop fighting with their technology and start using it to actually solve problems. I spend my days looking at messy workflows and trying to build cleaner, more efficient paths forward.

### Why this site?

I've always been a fan of the "minimal" approach—both in code and in business. I'm using this space to document:

* **Insights** on digital shifts and strategy.
* **Notes** on the tools and systems I'm currently testing.
* **Learnings** as I continue to tinker with web tech (like this Astro site).

### What's next?

I'm keeping things simple. No newsletters, no complex tracking—just thoughts on digital strategy and the occasional deep dive into a project I'm working on.

If you’re interested in how technology changes the way we work, I hope you'll find something useful here. 

Stay tuned.
